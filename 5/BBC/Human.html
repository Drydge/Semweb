Human

Humans are in the same great ape family as the chimpanzees, gorillas and orangutans - with which they share a common ancestor. Modern humans spread from their African homeland some 70,000 years ago and have come to dominate every continent on Earth except Antarctica. As a behaviourally, socially and culturally diverse species, humans have developed art and complex languages. As a species humans cannot survive without tools. They are the only known species to build fires, cook food and clothe themselves.

Scientific name: Homo sapiens
